Proposal for Work-From-Home Internship Program by JAE Hub

Introduction:

We are excited to present a comprehensive proposal for the Work-From-Home Internship Program offered by JAE Hub. Our commitment to providing valuable remote internship opportunities in various fields across the Philippines remains unwavering. In this proposal, we will outline the key aspects of our program, including the mode of payment and schedule.

We understand the importance of flexibility and transparency in our payment structure. For our WorkFrom-Home Internship Program, we offer the following mode of payment:

Payment Schedule:

• 1st Payment: Amount of 750 pesos. This initial payment is required before the commencement of your On-the-Job Training (OJT) or internship. It covers the enrollment and preparatory phase for your internship, ensuring you are ready to embark on this exciting journey.

• 2nd Payment: Amount of 750 pesos. The second payment is scheduled for after a one month of your OJT. This delay in payment allows you to assess your progress and commitment to the program before proceeding with the second installment. We value your satisfaction and want to ensure that you're confident in your decision to continue.

As part of our commitment to transparency and continuous improvement, we encourage you to review our Facebook page (JAE Hub Review). We take pride in the feedback we receive, and it serves as a testament to our dedication to empowering individuals while contributing to meaningful initiatives across the Philippines.
